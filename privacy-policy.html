Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12 July 2026

Beyond The Visa (“the App”, “we”, “us”) helps internationally relocating nurses and midwives organise professional registration, learning, relocation and career preparation. This notice explains the information used by the current web preview and the additional processing that applies when optional online services are connected.

1. Who is responsible

The service operator for Beyond The Visa is the data controller. Before commercial or app-store launch, the operator must add its full legal name, registered address and monitored privacy email here. Until then, privacy enquiries can be submitted through Contact Us → Privacy or data request.

2. Information used

  • Account details: name, email and authentication information managed by Supabase.
  • Professional profile: profession, specialty, qualification country, destination, registration stage, job status, dependants, profile photo and optional goals.
  • Uploaded files: certificates, passport or identity copies, visa documents, CVs and images that the user chooses to place in the private document vault.
  • Journey and learning: checklist progress, deadlines, notes, budgets, CBT/NCLEX answers, scores, topics and streaks.
  • Career and service requests: saved job searches, coaching or CV requests and contact messages.
  • Community and feedback: posts, replies, likes, ratings, bug reports and feature requests.
  • AI conversations: questions, recent conversation and relevant saved context sent only when a secure Zibur endpoint is configured.
  • Technical context: app version and browser information included with a bug report when the user submits it.

3. Storage

Some preferences and progress remain in browser local storage. Account information and optional user uploads use Supabase. Uploaded files are placed in a private bucket under a folder belonging to the signed-in user. The App requests short-lived signed links when a user opens a private file. Clearing browser storage does not delete cloud files.

4. Online services and sharing

Supabase provides authentication, database and private file-storage infrastructure. If the secure Zibur backend is connected, questions and relevant context are sent over HTTPS to the configured service and OpenAI to generate an answer. External links—including regulators, governments, Trac and trusted support sites—have their own privacy practices. We do not sell personal information.

5. Why information is used

Information is used to create and protect an account, personalise the selected country pathway, save progress, calculate estimates, provide learning feedback, enable requested community and support functions, answer Zibur questions, improve reliability and meet legal obligations. Before production launch, the operator must document the applicable lawful basis for each activity, including contract, legitimate interests, consent and legal obligation where relevant.

6. Health and immigration information

The App provides education and planning support. It is not a medical record, diagnostic service, immigration adviser, tax adviser or emergency service. Users may store their own immigration documents in the vault, but must not upload identifiable patient information, payment-card details, documents belonging to another person without authority or confidential employer records.

7. Retention and deletion

Local information remains until browser data is cleared or Delete account and local data is used. Cloud files remain until the user deletes them individually in My documents or submits a verified account-deletion request. The local deletion control does not by itself delete cloud files. Formal production retention periods and a complete verified account-erasure process must be published before commercial launch.

8. Security

Uploaded files use a private Supabase bucket with access rules restricting each signed-in user to their own folder. File links are short-lived. We use HTTPS and avoid placing private service keys in the browser. No service is completely secure, so users should upload only files needed for the stated purpose and delete them when no longer required.

9. International processing

Connected providers may process information in countries outside the user’s country. Before launch, the operator must document providers, locations and appropriate safeguards.

10. Children

The App is intended for adults preparing for professional nursing or midwifery careers and is not directed to children.

11. Your rights

Depending on location, users may have rights to access, correct, erase, restrict or object to processing, receive a portable copy, withdraw consent and complain to a regulator. The in-app export and deletion controls cover local preview data; production requests will require identity verification.

12. Changes and contact

Material changes will be dated and presented clearly. Submit questions through Contact Us and choose “Privacy or data request”.
