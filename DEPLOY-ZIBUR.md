<h2>Cookie & Local Storage Policy</h2><p><b>Last updated: ${legalUpdated}</b></p>
<p>The current Beyond The Visa preview does not set advertising or analytics cookies. It uses browser local storage—a similar device-storage technology—to remember information needed for requested features.</p>
<h3>What is stored locally</h3><ul><li>Account and session state</li><li>Country, profession and personalisation choices</li><li>Journey, costs, learning scores, streaks and deadlines</li><li>Theme, voice and interface preferences</li><li>Saved job searches, community preview posts and feedback</li><li>Optional secure Zibur endpoint and recent conversation context</li><li>Cookie-notice acknowledgement</li></ul>
<h3>Why it is used</h3><p>This storage is necessary to make the static preview remember the user’s choices and progress. It is not used for behavioural advertising.</p>
<h3>Managing storage</h3><p>Users can export or delete App data in <b>Privacy & Legal</b>. Browser controls can also clear site data, but doing so removes the local account and progress. External linked websites may use their own cookies and should be checked separately.</p>
<h3>Future analytics or marketing</h3><p>Non-essential analytics, personalisation or marketing technologies must not be enabled without updating this policy and, where required, obtaining prior consent through a genuine preference control.
