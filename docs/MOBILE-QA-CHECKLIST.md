# Mobile release QA checklist

## Required before store submission

- [ ] Sign up, email login, password reset, logout and Google login on physical Android and iPhone devices
- [ ] Zibur/Gemini responses, IELTS marking and graceful offline/error behavior
- [ ] Profile editing and image upload
- [ ] Learning routes: CBT, IELTS, NCLEX, OSCE, calculations, articles and study planner
- [ ] Journey, visa/passport and progress persistence across devices
- [ ] Jobs and external links
- [ ] Paystack test payment, verified Premium unlock and restore after reinstall
- [ ] Light/dark mode contrast and Learn content visibility
- [ ] Small phone, large phone, iPhone safe areas, Android gesture navigation and tablets
- [ ] Camera, share, downloads, deep link and push opt-in/denial
- [ ] Airplane mode, slow network, app background/resume and process termination
- [ ] Android accessibility scan, iOS VoiceOver, dynamic text and 44-point touch targets
- [ ] Privacy labels/data safety form, account deletion path and support contact
- [ ] Play internal testing and TestFlight external beta

No release should be called production-ready until these device and store checks are completed.
